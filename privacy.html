PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

Klabberjass is a single-player offline card game. Your privacy is simple: we don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

Data Collection

Klabberjass does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or internet connection to function.

Local Storage

The app stores your game settings, progress, and statistics locally on your device using standard on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Analytics & Tracking

Klabberjass does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. There are no third-party services integrated into the app.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at klabberjassMasters@gmail.com.
